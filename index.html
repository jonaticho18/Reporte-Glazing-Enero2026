What these numbers mean

This was a top of funnel campaign optimized for profile visits and follower growth. It is strong on reach, CTR, and engagement efficiency, but it does not yet measure purchases or revenue.
  • CAC and LTV are not available yet because this campaign does not track customer conversions.
  • To measure conversion rate and ROAS, we need a website or funnel event such as lead form submissions, bookings, or purchases.
  • Your target formula is spend one euro to get five euros or ideally fifty euros back, which requires revenue attribution.

Next actions for February

  • Scale AD4, keep AD1 as backup creative.
  • Refresh one new creative monthly to avoid fatigue.
  • Set up conversion tracking, Meta Pixel and key events.
  • Add a monthly tab to track trends over time.
